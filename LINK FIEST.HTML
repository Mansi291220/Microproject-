WELCOME TO FASHOIN INDUSTRY

The fashion industryconsists of four levels: the production of raw materials,
principally fibres and textiles but also leather and fur; the production of fashion goods by designers,
manufacturers, contractors, and others; retail sales; and various forms of advertising and promotion.
The industry provides employment opportunities to millions of people worldwide, from designers and
artisans to retail workers and logistic experts.
